WEBSITES

Wildzie

Wildzie is a vibrant and dynamic online shop dedicated to selling unique and trendy t-shirts. Built on WordPress, this e-commerce website showcases a wide range of t-shirt designs, catering to diverse tastes and styles. The site integrates WooCommerce for seamless product management and online transactions, ensuring a smooth shopping experience.

Design Process:

For Wildzie, the design journey began with wireframing in Adobe XD. I created detailed wireframes to map out the user journey and layout for both desktop and mobile views. These wireframes served as blueprints, ensuring a logical flow and a consistent user experience across devices. Using Adobe XD allowed me to visualize the structure and interactivity of the website, making it easier to refine the user interface and design elements before development.

ZeStyle

ZeStyle is another creative venture into the world of online t-shirt retail, developed using Wix. This platform allowed for quick deployment and easy customization, making it ideal for showcasing a range of vibrant and trendy t-shirts.
